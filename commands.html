Commands:

  • -help

    Shows the bot's commands.

  • -play

    Starts playing the music you want.

  • -stop

    Turns off the music you are listening to.

  • -pause

    Pauses the music you are listening to.

  • -resume

    Resumes the music you are listening to.

  • -skip

    Skips to next song.

  • -back

    Goes back to the song you passed.

  • -search

    You search for a song.

  • -volume

    You adjust the volume of the song.

  • -time

    Shows the time of the song.

  • -nowplaying

    Provides information about the song you are listening to.

  • -queue

    Shows the song queue.

  • -save

    It saves the song you are listening to and sends it to you as a private message.

  • -clear

    Clears the song queue.

  • -loop

    You can loop the song.

  • -filter

    You can add filter to the song.
